StoryTailor Design Assistant — an internal creative studio tool

The Office is a creative and branding studio operated by Yoav Halevy. The StoryTailor Design Assistant is our internal tool that helps our design team gather and organize visual inspiration for client and brand projects.

What the app does

After the account owner authorizes it through Pinterest's official OAuth flow, the app reads our own Pinterest boards and pins and organizes them into a private design-reference library. It is used only by our studio, for our own creative work.

What it does not do

  • It does not post, create, or modify any content on Pinterest.
  • It does not access other users' Pins or Boards.
  • It does not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.
