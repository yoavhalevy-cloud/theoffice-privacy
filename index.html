Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Office ("we", "us", "our"), a creative studio operated by Yoav Halevy, handles information in connection with our internal design-reference application (the "Application") that integrates with the Pinterest API.

1. Purpose of the Application

The Application is an internal tool used by our studio to read design inspiration from our own Pinterest boards and pins. Its sole purpose is to help our design team collect and organize visual references for our creative work. The Application is not offered to the general public and does not create a service for third-party end users.

2. Information We Access

Through the Pinterest API, and only after the account owner authorizes access via Pinterest's official OAuth flow, the Application may read: board names, pin images, pin descriptions, and related public metadata belonging to the connected account. We do not collect Pinterest login credentials, passwords, or session cookies.

3. How We Use the Information

Accessed information is used exclusively to build an internal library of design references. We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose this information to any third party. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to our own internal creative work.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Any references saved by the Application are stored locally within our own working environment. Access tokens are stored securely and are used only to make authorized API requests. You may revoke the Application's access at any time from your Pinterest account settings, which immediately ends its ability to read your data.

5. Third Parties

The Application communicates only with the official Pinterest API. We do not transmit accessed data to any other external service.

6. Your Rights

Because the Application accesses only the connected account owner's own Pinterest content, that owner retains full control. You may disconnect the Application and request deletion of any stored references at any time by contacting us.

7. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: yoavhalevy@gmail.com.
